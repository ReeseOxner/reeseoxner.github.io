I love the <marquee>.

For the uninitiated, the marquee tag is a no-longer-to-be-used HTML tag that creates scrolling text.

That small piece of code is the reason I learned front-end development. It also spurred a love for online communities that informed many parts of my life. HOW NICKELODEON TAUGHT ME TO CODE


On Nick.com Message Boards, there were several boards for discussion, mostly revolving around the various Nickelodeon franchises. But my favorite boards were the ones centered on social topics.
